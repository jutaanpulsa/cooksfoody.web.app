##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Salad:

1. Make ready 230 gm Canned tuna (packed in water)  after draining
1. Prepare 1/4 cup Celery stalks  diced
1. Make ready 4 tbsp Onion diced
1. Prepare 3 tbsp Green capsicum diced
1. Make ready 1/4 cup Carrot  grated
1. Make ready 3 tbsp Cucumber (deseeded)  diced
1. Get 1 tsp Red chilli  finely minced
1. Make ready  Coriander leaves - handful, finely chopped
1. Prepare 2 tsp Sweet mango chutney
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp Garlic powder
1. Make ready 2. 1/2 tbsp Parmesan cheese
1. Take 1 tsp Red chilli flakes
1. Take 1/2 tsp Black pepper powder
1. Make ready 3/4 tbsp Whole grain mustard
1. Take 6-8 tbsp Mayonnaise
1. Take 2 tbsp Lemon juice
1. Prepare to taste Salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Tuna Salad:

1. Using a fork, break the tuna into flakes.
1. In a mixing bowl add all the ingredients and mix until well combined.
1. Spoon into a salad bowl.
1. Cover with cling wrap and refrigerate until needed.
