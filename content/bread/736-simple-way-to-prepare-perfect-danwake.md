##### Instructions to make Danwake:

1. Add water to form a paste (not too thick, not too watery)
1. Put a pot on fire, add water and bring to a boil
1. Once the water starts boiling, cut the paste into small or medium sized balls to the water, do so for the rest of the paste and allow to boil.
1. Bring down, sieve and add to a plate
1. Garnish, serve and enjoy
