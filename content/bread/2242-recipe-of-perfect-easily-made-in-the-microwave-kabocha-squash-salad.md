##### The ingredients needed to make Easily Made in the Microwave!! Kabocha Squash Salad:

1. Make ready 1 net 250 grams Kabocha squash (peeled or unpeeled, to taste)
1. Prepare 60 grams Mayonnaise
1. Get 2 thin sausages Fish sausages (or bacon, wieners, etc)
1. Prepare 3 Cheese squares
1. Get 40 grams Steamed kuromame (or edamame)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Easily Made in the Microwave!! Kabocha Squash Salad:

1. Cut the kabocha into bite-sized chunks, place in a heatproof dish, add a little water, and microwave at 1000 W for 5 minutes. Adjust the time according to your microwave.
1. While waiting for it to cook in the microwave, cut the sausages and cheese into chunks the size of your beans.
1. When the kabocha is easily pierced with a skewer, drain the water and mash while still hot.
1. Mix with the mayonnaise, then mix in the other ingredients. It&#39;s done!!
