##### The ingredients needed to make Black Eyed Peas Tikka Masala:

1. Make ready 3 1/2 tsp Tikka Masala seasoning mix
1. Make ready 3 tsp Curry powder
1. Prepare 3 tsp turmeric powder
1. Make ready 10 clove garlic
1. Get 1 can diced green chiles
1. Prepare 1 large onion
1. Make ready 1/2 cup chopped cilantro
1. Take 1 packages dried black eyed peas
1. Prepare 1 can diced tomatoes
1. Get 1 cup heavy cream
1. Get 4 cup water
1. Take 1 smoked pork hamhock (optional)
1. Take 1/3 cup olive oil
1. Get 3 tsp cumin powder




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Black Eyed Peas Tikka Masala:

1. Soak peas overnight in water. Drain them before cooking.
1. In a food processor combine onion, garlic,  cilantro. Pulse it until everything is finely chopped.
1. In a large pot (I used a 6qt Dutch oven)? put the oil in it &amp; cook the onion/garlic/cilantro mix with the green chiles &amp; the canned diced tomatoes.
1. Add the cumin, curry, tumeric, &amp; Tikka masala seasonings. Let it cook for about another 5 minutes to let the flavors blend.
1. Add the black eyed peas, smoked pork hamhock, &amp; water. Salt &amp; pepper to taste,  if you want.
1. Once it comes to a boil on med-high heat, cover &amp; let it simmer on med-low until peas are soft &amp; the &#34;sauce&#34; thickens. (About 2.5 hours)
1. Add heavy cream &amp; let it summer uncovered for another 30 - 40 minutes.
1. Once it is nice &amp; thick, you are done! I served this over white jasmine rice &amp; it was delicious!
