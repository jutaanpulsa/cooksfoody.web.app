##### The ingredients needed to make Peas and white rice:

1. Prepare 1 tsp paprika
1. Take 3 pieces carrots(grated)
1. Make ready to taste Salt
1. Get  C/oil
1. Take 1 onion
1. Take 300 g rice (boiled)

##### Steps to make Peas and white rice:
