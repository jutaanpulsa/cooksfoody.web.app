##### Instructions to make Shrikhand:

1. You can use either homemade curd or store bought curd according to the avilabillity.use the curd that is not soure.
1. Take a deep and board bowl and place a large strainer on it.spread a clean muslin cloth and cheese cloth over it and the plain curd in the center. warp the muslin cloth from all side and tight tie it.
1. Hang curd in a refrigerator for about 6to 7 hour or until almost water is drained of from curd.place an empty container below it to collect the whey.curd should becom very thick.
1. After 6-7 hour untie the muslin cloth and transfer thik strained curd to a bowl.
1. Add sugar powder and cardamom power, mix properly using a spoon until smooth.
1. Add chopped badam and cashews and mix well.
1. Serve it chilled.
