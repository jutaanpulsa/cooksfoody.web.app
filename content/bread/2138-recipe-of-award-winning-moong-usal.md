##### Instructions to make Moong usal:

1. Wash whole moong and soak for 6 hours minimum. Later drain water and take in a napkin tie up and keep it in a hotpot/caserole for whole day to make sprouts.
1. In a kadhai take oil add mustard, cumin, hing, turmeric.after they spluutter add curry leaves
1. Now add moong sprouts and peanuts. Mix well add red chilli powder
1. Now add 4 cups water and salt and sugar. cover lids and cook for 10 mins.
1. They are cooked and ready to serve
