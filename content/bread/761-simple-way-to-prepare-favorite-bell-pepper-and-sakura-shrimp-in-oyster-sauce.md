##### The ingredients needed to make Bell Pepper and Sakura Shrimp in Oyster Sauce:

1. Take 4 Green bell peppers
1. Prepare 10 grams Sakura shrimp (small)
1. Make ready 1/2 tbsp Sesame oil
1. Make ready 1 tsp ◆Oyster sauce
1. Make ready 1 tsp ◆Sake
1. Get 1/4 tsp ◆Sugar
1. Make ready 1 tsp Mirin
1. Prepare 1/2 tbsp Toasted sesame (white)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Bell Pepper and Sakura Shrimp in Oyster Sauce:

1. Slice the bell peppers diagonally.
1. Heat sesame oil in a pan and lightly fry the shrimp. Add peppers and fry some more. Then, add the ◆ ingredients and fry until the liquid in the pan has evaporated.
1. Drizzle mirin and add your favourite seasonings when it has become shiny. Add the sesame seeds, mix quickly, and it&#39;s done.
