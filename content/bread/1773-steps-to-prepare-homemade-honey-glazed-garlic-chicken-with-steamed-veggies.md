##### The ingredients needed to make Honey Glazed Garlic Chicken with steamed veggies:

1. Take 4 Chicken thigh and leg pieces
1. Take 1 tsp Soya sauce
1. Make ready 1 tsp Vinegar
1. Prepare to taste Salt
1. Prepare 3 tsp Honey
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp Pepper powder
1. Get 2 tbsp Garlic paste
1. Get as needed Steamed veggies (carrots, beans)
1. Prepare 2 tsp Lemon juice
1. Make ready 2 tbsp Olive oil




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Honey Glazed Garlic Chicken with steamed veggies:

1. Marinate the chicken pieces with salt, pepper powder, 1tbspgarlic, lemon juice and Keep overnight.
1. Heat oil in a frying pan. Add the marinated chicken pieces and fry on high heat for sometime.
1. Reduce flame and add the remaining garlic paste, pepper powder, soya sauce,vinegar,salt and stir and add little water and cook covered till chicken pieces are cooked.
1. At last drizzle the honey and mix and remove.
1. Serve with steamed veggies of your choice.
