##### The ingredients needed to make Peas Soup:

1. Prepare 1 cup green peas (fresh or frozen)
1. Take 1 onion, small diced
1. Take 1 clove garlic, minced
1. Make ready 1 cup milk
1. Take 3-4 cashews
1. Get 1 tsp butter
1. Make ready to taste Salt and black powder




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Peas Soup:

1. In a pot of water add peas to boil. Once they are tender add them to a large bowl with ice and water.  -  - This will retain their green colour
1. Let it sit in the water for 5 minutes then drain and set aside.
1. In a pan add some butter, put a bay leaf for flavor, sauté onions and garlic. Stir occasionally
1. Now add cashews and boiled peas to it, sauté for 2-3 minutes more -  - Add half of the milk to avoid burning
1. Boil for 2-3 minutes and switch off the flame -  - Let it cool naturally
1. Now remove the bay leaf and blend the soup
1. You can sieve the soup and pour again in the pan to reheat and season it
1. Add salt and black pepper powder
1. Add more milk to adjust to the thick consistency
