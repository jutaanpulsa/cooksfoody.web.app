##### The ingredients needed to make Orange Cucumber Salad:

1. Take 1 Ripe Sweet Orange
1. Make ready 1 Salad Cucumber
1. Get 1 Carrot
1. Take 25 grams Raisins
1. Prepare 1 tablespoon lime juice
1. Prepare 1/4 tablespoon Kashmiri Chilli Powder
1. Take 1/4 tablespoon Salt
1. Prepare 25 grams Walnuts (Optional)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Orange Cucumber Salad:

1. Take the pith and peel off from each orange segment and cut into two
1. Chop the cucumber and carrots into bite size pieces
1. Add salt, chilli powder and lemon juice.
1. Add the raisins and toss the salad
1. Refrigerate for 30 minutes and Enjoy your salad!
