##### The ingredients needed to make Babi Hong:

1. Take 400 g pork belly (strips)
1. Take 2 tbsp dark soy sauce
1. Get 2 tbsp oyster sauce
1. Take 2 tbsp shaoxing wine
1. Get 3 tbsp sugar
1. Get  Salt and pepper
1. Get  Sayur asin item (soaked)
1. Get 4 cloves garlic (chopped)
1. Prepare 2 star anise seed
1. Prepare to taste Ginger
1. Make ready 1 Green onion
1. Get as needed Cornstarch




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Babi Hong:

1. Pan fry the pork till cooked
1. Cut the pork strips to 4 cm long and marinate with dark soy, oyster sauce and sugar, set aside for a while
1. Stir fry the vegetables with garlic and the excess marinade liquid then add the star anise
1. Place the pork belly in the bowl and put the vegetables and sliced green onion and ginger on top then steam for 1 hour
1. After it&#39;s done take the star anise, ginger, and green onion away
1. Put the steamed pork on a plate and take the juice to a pan and thicken it with cornstarch
1. Pour the thickened sauce on top of the pork and enjoy with rice
