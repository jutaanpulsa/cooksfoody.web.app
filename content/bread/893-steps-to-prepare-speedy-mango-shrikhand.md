##### The ingredients needed to make Mango Shrikhand:

1. Prepare 1 kg curd
1. Prepare 1/2 mango puree
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
1. Make ready 1/4 powdered sugar
1. Get 1 pinch saffron
1. Make ready 1 tbsp mango pieces




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Mango Shrikhand:

1. Take a muslin cloth and spread it on a big bowl. - Pour the curd in the cloth. - Now bring the ends of the cloth together and hang it on a support to drain the water from the curd. - Let the curd hang for 2-3 hours.
1. Mix the hungcurd with the remaining ingredients. - Whisk nicely to make it smooth. - Transfer in the serving bowls.
1. Garnish with mango pieces &amp; serve as it
