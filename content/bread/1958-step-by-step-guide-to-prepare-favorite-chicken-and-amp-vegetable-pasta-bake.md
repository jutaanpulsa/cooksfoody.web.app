##### Steps to make Chicken &amp; Vegetable Pasta Bake:

1. Cook the pasta per the instructions on the packet and set aside
1. Add the olive oil to a pan over a medium heat - Add in the garlic, chilli and chicken
1. Sauté for 5 minutes - Add in the onion, peppers, broccoli and mushrooms
1. Sauté for 5 minutes or until the vegetables are tender - Pour in the cream and season to taste
1. Pour in the cream and season to taste - Cook for a couple of minutes
1. Stir in 50g of the dairy free cheese - Heat the grill to high
1. Stir the cooked pasta into the sauce - Pour into a large casserole dish and top with the rest of the grated cheese
